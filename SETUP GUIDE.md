
Welcome to my **AI second brain 2.0 template**!

Follow along the steps to get all set up.

> VIDEO GUIDE: https://youtu.be/4l8MXYUqGaA

#### STEP 0: 

Make sure you have **Claude Code** installed in your terminal & you're logged in to your acc with pro or higher. 

> Follow steps here: https://code.claude.com/docs/en/quickstart

> FOR MAC: Open Terminal and paste this `curl -fsSL https://claude.ai/install.sh | bash`

OR you can use the **Claude desktop app** and choose this folder as your project workspace.

*(Same with any other IDE: Antigravity, VS Code, Warp, Etc)*

> Run claude in your terminal, then tell him 'help me install this repo https://github.com/YishenTu/claudian'

Restart Obsidian, change your 'Claudian' plugin settings in the Obsidian settings.

#### STEP 1: 

You can start the automated setup by telling claude:

> *'run the brain-setup skill in the 05 skills folder'*

He'll interview you and fill in the CLAUDE.md file.

#### STEP 2:

Next you'll want to take some time to journal about your life goals and put your notes in the GOALS file.

*(Or ask claude to interview you about your goals and he can fill it in based on your answers.)*

#### STEP 3:

Start a new Obsidian session ('Open folder as a vault') in the  `03 Projects > (PROJECT TEMPLATE)`Folder

> Customize the appearance, settings, and plugins of Obsidian and now every project you create will have the same plugins and appearance.


#### STEP 4:

> Tell claude to 'run the new-project skill from the 05 skills folder' 

He'll walk you through setting up you're first project!

*(If you're using the claudian obsidian plugin, make sure to reconfigure the settings in each project.)*

#### STEP 5:

Use claude in this layer to clarify your goals and plan what you need to do to get there!

*(I highly recommend thinking through your 'chess moves' on your own first THEN getting claude to analyze and think through them with you)*

Open an obsidian vault in each individual project to build those systems, run them, and hit your goals. 📈


#### STEP 6:

> Use the 'weekly-review' skill in the 05 Skills folder at the end of each week to update all of your CLAUDE.md's and keep everything up to date.


#### STEP 7:

Customize to YOU.

The power of this second brain is that it can be **personalized completely** to YOU and YOUR projects/workflows.

This takes time but is extremely worth it.

> Customize your obsidian plugins
> Create your own skills
> Bookmark stuff
> Customize your CLAUDE.md's

Do whatever will make this valuable to YOU.

#### BONUS STEP 1: (Mobile 2nd Brain - Claude Dispatch)

Download Claude Desktop App & Claud Phone App

Run desktop app, go to 'Dispatch'

Open your phone app and connect.

> Say this to claude: I want you to use an already existing folder on my computer at ~/Desktop/(YOUR 2ND BRAIN FOLDER NAME). Set that as your permanent workspace so you always work from there.

- Tip: Turn notification on your phone for Claude.
- Tip: Any frequent tasks you do, you might wanna add that to the Layer 1 Claude.md to help him execute it easily. *(My EX: Saving video ideas into my 2nd channel project from my phone)*

> Now ask him to do whatever you need in your 2nd brain from your phone! From ANYWHERE!!!! Crazy time to be alive.



#### BONUS STEP 2: (Team 2nd Brain/Projects - Obsidian Sync)

> You can sign up for obsidian sync here: https://obsidian.md/sync 

Then go to your setting in Obsidian:
- Settings > Core Plugins > Sync > TURN ON

Then go back to your setting, choose 'Sync' on the left hand side
- Choose the remote vault to sync to
- You can then click on the little 'people' icon to sync with (they'll also need sync) add their obsidian email.
