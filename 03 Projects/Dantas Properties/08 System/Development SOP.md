This is the living step-by-step tracker for how a development project moves from raw site to leased, stabilized asset. Copy the checklist below into a new note per active project (in the matching stage folder) and update it as the project moves through stages.

## Process Stages

1. **Site Identification** (`00 Site Identification/`)
   - Identify and evaluate the property/site

2. **Site Plan** (`01 Site Plan/`)
   - Move through the site plan process

3. **Permits & Approvals** (`02 Permits & Approvals/`)
   - Get permits and approvals with the city

4. **Development & Construction** (`03 Development & Construction/`)
   - Build and develop the property

5. **Certificate of Occupancy & Grants** (`04 Certificate of Occupancy & Grants/`)
   - Get certificate of occupancy
   - Submit for the Enterprise Grant

6. **Marketing & Leasing** (`05 Marketing & Leasing/`)
   - Begin marketing the completed space

7. **Leased Portfolio** (`06 Leased Portfolio/`)
   - Done — property is leased and part of the stabilized portfolio
