# CVA CCSC — Q2 — June 11, 2026 @ 10:00 AM

**Context:** Multi-company safety roundtable addressing an uptick in vehicle/field incidents — camera use, coaching practices, fatigue, PPE lifecycle.

**Key takeaways:**
- Samsara camera deployment alone didn't reduce incidents as expected; some drivers feel anxious about being recorded — better results come from pairing footage with non-punitive coaching.
- AWP's model: a video repository of safety clips for frontline leader coaching, plus a monthly non-punitive "crew coaching" review.
- Root causes identified: micro-sleep/fatigue in severe incidents, complacency and poor task planning near equipment (per recent **Miller Pipeline** field injuries).
- PPE: boot voucher pilot to replace worn footwear and reduce ankle-roll/puncture risk.
- Positive recognition (food, praise using Samsara safety scores) considered essential to sustaining safe behavior.

**Action items / ideas to consider adopting at Mr. Pave:**
- Structured coaching form: video review + performance metrics + 5 employee-written improvement commitments.
- Five-whys documentation for incidents.
- Site-specific pre-job briefings (not generic/repetitive).
- Consider a boot voucher program.
